☵ Avradeep Bhowmik

I am PhD candidate at The University of Texas at Austin with a research focus on Machine Learning, Data Science and related areas (expected graduation: Summer 2018). I am supervised by Prof. Joydeep Ghosh, and have been affiliated with the Intelligent Data Exploration and Analysis Laboratory (IDEA Lab) and the Wireless Networking and Communications Group (WNCG) since August 2013.

I received my Master of Science (M.S.) degree from the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at UT Austin in 2016. Prior to graduate school, I received a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech.) in Electrical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay (IIT Bombay) in 2013. At IIT Bombay, I worked on my undergraduate thesis under the supervision of Prof. Vivek Borkar.


I am currently in the job market and actively looking for full time positions with a focus on machine learning, data mining/analytics, and related areas.



☵ Research

I am broadly interested in Machine Learning, Data Mining, Optimization, Statistical Inference and related fields. My thesis focuses on developing techniques and algorithms for learning from data that is only available in aggregated form or partially observed form due to constraints arising from privacy preservation, scalability, proprietary concerns, robustness, etc. I have further worked in problems involving compressed sensing, ranking, rank and search aggregation, sparse modelling, etc. and have hands-on experience in working on industry projects involving advertising technology, search, ranking and recommendation systems among others.
